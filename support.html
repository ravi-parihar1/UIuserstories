Frequently Asked Questions

📅 What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 24 hours before check-in, 50% refund if cancelled 12-24 hours before, no refund for cancellations less than 12 hours before check-in.

🕐 What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.

🐕 Are pets allowed?

Yes, pets are allowed with an additional fee of $25 per night. Please inform us in advance if you're bringing a pet.

🅿️ Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all guests. Valet parking is also available for $15 per day.

🍳 Is breakfast included?

Continental breakfast is included in all room rates. Full breakfast buffet is available for an additional $15 per person.
